説明:

Eastwood 2.75" Contour DSB Dustless Sandpaper rolls are a premium aluminum oxide abrasive sandpaper featuring a specialized latex paper backing. This DSB paper offers greater flexibility, resistance to cracking, excellent edge wear, extended service life and a consistent finish. Holes in paper align with the holes in the sanding blocks and the vacuum technology not only collects more than 95% of the dust but also greatly extends paper life by preventing clogging and paper loading.

Eastwood DSB Dustless Sandpaper is a PSA (pressure sensitive adhesive) backing for the Eastwood DSB Sanding Blocks. This flexible abrasive sandpaper is for dry sanding only and is great not only for large flat surfaces but also for sanding shapes, edges, and contours. Keep your garage and yourself clean when using the Eastwood DSB Dustless sanding kit that allows you to get laser straight panels without the mess!
