[楽譜] パスファインダース・マーチ(スパーク) 吹奏楽譜【送料無料】(Pathfinders March)《輸入楽譜》

ジャンル：オリジナル(輸入)

弊社に在庫がない場合の取り寄せ発送目安：8日〜31日

グレード：1

解説：Composer Philip Sparke has long been fascinated by history’s pioneers. In all fields of human activity， there has been someone with enormous vision who has made the first step into the unknown. Explorers have found new lands， doctors have discovered new medicines and scientists are pushing the bounds of technology ever further. But there was always one ‘pathfinder’， someone who was not afraid of the unknown， who dared to go one step further than anyone before him. The march is dedicated those pioneers who have risked everything for the sake of their vision.
