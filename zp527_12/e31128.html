説明: Bluetooth keyboard connection steps：
1: After turning the power switch to the ON position, the power indicator light will be on for 3 seconds. Press Fn+C to enter the code matching mode, and the bluetooth indicator light will start to flash. Then the bluetooth keyboard automatically enter the pairing mode.
2：Open and unlock your tablet and click on the " Settings” icon .
3 . In the settings menu , click on the“ Bluetooth ”menu.
4 . Turn on the Bluetooth switch and the tablet will automatically search for Bluetooth keyboard devices.
5 . Discover the Bluetooth keyboard device: Bluetooth Keyboard * * ,and click on it ,the Bluetooth keyboard will automatically connect.
6 . After the Bluetooth connection is successful , the pairing indicator is off ,and " Connected " will appear in the list of Bluetooth devices.
