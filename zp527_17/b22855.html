　Ducati or our carbon fiber hand-guard support kit for Ducati Multistrada V4， which allows the replacement of the original clutch master cylinder with the more high-performance Brembo RCS and RCS CorsaCorta radial clutch master cyclinder.
The kit consists of
- a polyamide bracket with chemical smoothing and immersion coloring：the treatment gives very high mechanical resistance and to atmospheric agents.
- a fixing screw made of light alloy， machined from solid and then anodized.
- a pin made of light alloy， machined from solid and then anodized， which replaces the Brembo master cylinder one.
