Refueling Tips for Eccentric Pneumatic Grinder: 
1. This machine is highly accurate. When it is running, the Speed is high. Once any fine sand or iron filings enter the movement, it may cause the blade to break and cause the machine to malfunction, so make sure that the air inlet is clean.
2. Be sure to use an oil-water separator and drain regularly to ensure that the machine is dry.
3. Before use every day, be sure to add 3~5 drops of pneumatic oil to the air inlet of the machine, and connect the air pump to idle.

Need to Know for Changing Disc of Eccentric Pneumatic Grinder:
