A tip for tuning your new Ukulele:
Are you finding your new ukulele always needs tuning every time you want to play it?
This is really common, particularly with new strings. Nylon strings take a certain amount of stretching before they settle down and hold
their tune.
How to solve:Tune it to the desired note, then pull up on the string over the sound hole to give it a bit of a stretch. That"ll take it down
about 2 whole notes, tune it back up and repeat until it stays in tune. A lot of folks who maybe aren"t used to stringed
instruments, especially nylon strings, get frustrated, but don"t worry, these are decent strings, they just need a good stretch
