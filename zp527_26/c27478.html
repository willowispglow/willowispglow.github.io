【本の内容】
Denegar (U. of Connecticut) E. Saliba and S. Saliba"s (both U. of Virginia) updated text provides users with an understanding of the indications for modality applications and knowledge about how to safely apply modalities in a manner that promotes completion of a rehabilitation plan of care. Combining theoretical and practical information the text is suitable as a textbook for athletic training students and as a reference practicing for certified athletic trainers and other allied health care professionals. The revised material reflects new research findings since publication of the 2006 second edition. While the new edition contains expanded discussion of the effectiveness of specific interventions the main focus is on the information gathering and evaluation process used by clinicians to develop effective evidence-based treatment plans.
