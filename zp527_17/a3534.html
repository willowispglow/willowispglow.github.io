Leverage Six Sigma to Transform Product Design and Development
Today"s customer demand unprecedented reliability efficiency flexibility and affordability. Wang has transformed product design at companies ranging from Maytag and Visteon to General Electric. In this book he illuminates the full spectrum of proven techniques from Voice-of-Customer (VOC) and Critical-to-Quality (CTQ) to Kano modeling. Starting out: identifying projects organizing teams developing VOC models
