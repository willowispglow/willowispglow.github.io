    This Heavy l Suspensions kit is designed to provide an adjustable 1 inch to 2 inch rear drop for the 2004 - 2023 Nissan Titan (2WD 4WD). Our rear shackle kits are one of the fastest and easiest ways to change the rear ride height of your vehicle. Lift the rear of the vehicle off the ground, support the axle, and simply swap out your stock shackles with our new shackles. They were crafted from high strength steel and welded together through a computerized process to ensure accuracy and safety. Once the kit is installed, we do recommend getting an alignment to maintain your ride quality and fix any wondering that may occur. Each kit comes complete with installation instructions.
