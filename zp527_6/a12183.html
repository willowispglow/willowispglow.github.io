Available in six sizes, our Drybags enrich every motorcycle tour and offer a versatile luggage element with simple fastening technology. Thanks to the elastic PVC material, the bags can be easily rolled and transported compressed on the motorcycle regardless of the luggage load. The drybags are waterproof in the main compartment and are highly UV-resistant. The new arrangement of the straps allows easy combination with other Drybag models on the passenger seat or the rack. Thanks to their simple design, the new Drybags perfectly fit to the waterproof SysBag WP bags and the WP tank bags from the PRO line.
