説明: Description:
TFmini-S is the improved version of TFmini, it is a single-point micro ranging module and could be widely used in the integrated application of various drones, UAVs, Robots and industrial equipments. It is based on ToF (Time of Flight) principle and integrated with unique optical and electrical designs, so as to achieve stable, precise, high sensitivity and high-speed distance detection.
