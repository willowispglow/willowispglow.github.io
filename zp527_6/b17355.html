説明:
This constant voltage and constant currentprogrammable control power supply module makes analog integration and digitalcontrol functions combined in one. It has power-down storage function, which canstore 10 groups of preset value and quickly extract two groups of stored value.Compared with traditional power supply module, it is more convenient to quicklyextract the voltage or current required. Integrated with the function voltmeterand ammeter, it shows real time voltage and current clearly on the LCD screen. This module features small size, advanced function, goodvisual effect, strong operability, high accuracy, independent use, embeddeduse and more.
