How to install Water Pump
Begin by draining coolant, disconnecting hoses, and removing the decrepit pump. Thoroughly clean the mounting surface. Install the new pump, aligning with manufacturer guidelines. Attach hoses, refill coolant, and run the engine to check for leaks.

Function of Water Pump
The water pump circulates coolant throughout the engine, maintaining an excellent operating temperature. By avoiding overheating, it safeguards components from damage and ensures efficient combustion, enhancing overall vehicle performance.

Instruction of Water Pump
