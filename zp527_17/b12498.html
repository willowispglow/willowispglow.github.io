    The Best Choice for Active Speakers

    More and more audiophiles find that the behavior of normal multimedia speakers cannot meet their demands, and then they choose professional active monitor speakers. However, this kind of speakers usually doesn"t have a volume controller, which means a pre-amplifier is needed.

    Active monitor speakers are mostly placed on the desk and used for near field listening. So they need to consider the area required for Hi-Fi pre-amplifiers (normally very big). What"more, different pre-amplifiers may differ in their timbres and this is the most bothersome issue.

    As an audiophile brand, Nobsound decided to solve this problem and designed this product. Its small size won"t take much place and its clean silver color suits for various usage scenarios. There is no amplifying circuit in the device and won"t change the timbre, while the amplifier in the active speaker is enough to drive it. With this product, you can enjoy the most original timbre and quality of the speakers and won"t be trapped in the selection of pre-amplifiers.
