Religion Around Mary Shelley

著者名: Jennifer L. Airey 出版者: Pennsylvania State University Press 出版年: 31 Jul 2019 版表示: シリーズ: 5 HardbackExplores Mary Shelley as an important religious thinker of the Romantic period. Analyzes her creative engagement with contemporary religious controversies and uncovers a belief system that was both influenced by and profoundly different from those of her male Romantic counterparts.
