CLEANING - F. Dick products with plastic handles are dishwasher proof. In the long run, a change in color intensity, staining and rusting may occur. We recommended cleaning all chef’s knives by hand. F. Dick knives and sharpening steels are suitable for cleaning in sterilizers up to 130°C. Residues of highly concentrated washing agents, tools left in the washer for long periods of time and also egg white and meat residues can cause stains on the blade. Therefore, we recommend cleaning immediately with a mildly alkaline cleaning detergent. Store the knives and sharpening steels in a suitable knife holder, a sheath or in a dry location to avoid the blades hitting against each other.
