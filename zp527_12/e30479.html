Overview

Synvisus 2 port HDMI KVM Switch has a compact and elegant design for easy operation. It enables effortless control over 2 devices with HDMI video connections or computers using a single keyboard, mouse, and monitor, saving space on your desk and reducing equipment clutter. It"s HDCP 2.0 compliant and supports up to 4K@30Hz  resolutions. It can read and remember the monitor"s parameters, decreasing delays or changes in video resolution when switching between computers. Features an extra USB 2.0 port, it is perfect for connecting USB 2.0 peripherals such as an MP3 player, printer, scanner to the KVM Switch.
