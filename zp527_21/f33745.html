Instructions for using the bluetooth waterproof speaker:

1. After turning on the wireless speaker, you will hear the sound of "Bluetooth Mode" and the LED indicator will flash.

2. Open the Bluetooth settings in your phone.

3. After the connection is successful, you will hear a "Bluetooth connection successful" sound.

4. Long press + to increase the volume, and at the same time increase the volume of the phone to achieve the maximum volume effect, long press - to decrease the volume, you can adjust the appropriate volume according to the environment.

5. The flame lamp only has the function of a night light, which can only play the role of adjusting the atmosphere, not lighting, please understand.

6. How to turn it on or off: Press and hold the power button for about 3-5 seconds to turn on or off the Bluetooth speaker.
