【本の内容】
With its vibrant narrative engaging pedagogy currency and theoretical scholarship this text uses a wealth of multicultural and global examples to provide students with substantive coverage of sociological concepts. It integrates global analyzes throughout so that students learn to think critically about sociology as a discipline. New material on social diversity discusses differences among four cultural groups - Native Americans African Americans Hispanic Americans and Asian Americans - on various topics such as socialization aging education economics religion and politics.
